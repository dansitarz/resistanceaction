Resistance Action is a federal 501(c)4 non-profit organization, registered in the State of Illinois. We are dedicated to being a one-stop clearinghouse for easy access to membership, donations, and actions of progressive organizations across the United States. We also provide quick and easy access for finding townhall meetings, and contacting local, state, and federal elected and appointed officials and informing them of your concerns. Our mission is to make it easy for concerned citizens to turn their passion for protecting democracy, rights, and the environment into direct and positive action against the unprecedented attacks by the Trump administration. Our directors have many decades of experience as educators, financial officers, non-profit directors, entrepreneurs, and activists.


Any donations made to Resistance Action are fully tax-deductible and may be made through the link above which connects to ActBlue, the leading progressive fund-raising organization in America. All donations will be used to further the mission of Resistance Action.
